App Design - Home Chef

What is essential is invisible to the eye - it's my job to make you see with your heart.

Project Vision

In Asia, housewives are often looked down upon simply because they are not the bread winner, neglecting their contribution to the society by taking care of their family.

Home Chef is a community project aimed at raising the social status of women. By extending and commercialising their extraordinary ability to cook for their family, women can proudly prove that they are indispensable to the well-being of our society.

Home Chef is a mobile app for busy people to enjoy the bliss of cooking for their loved ones. Since ingredients are already processed by local housewives, cooking the preped food become quick and easy.
