MORE NOTES

Spend some extra time on the cover of the slide decks. You'll show this screen during prep time so make sure there's information people'd be interested in reading for say, 5 mins. By reading I don't mean text only - show visuals that would attract eyeballs. Make them look forward to the presentation you're about the give them.
