Landing Page Design

Landing pages are great tools for conversion-focused campaigns. Stand out with eye-catching visuals and impressive copies, accompanied with convincing testimonial.

Essential Eletments

In most cases, there are 3 essential elements on a landing page,

  • a) user input (ususally a form as easy as an email input field),
  • b) testimonial and
  • c) appealing visuals and extra-digestible copies.

UX writing, or microcopy, is a deciding factor for conversion rate in a design. I should probably write a separate page for this discipline later...when I've more time that is
